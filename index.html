About Us

CALIFORNIA'S PREMIER SHOWER DOOR SPECIALISTS

Since 2020, Merlord has been transforming California bathrooms with premium shower door solutions. We pride ourselves on offering the highest quality products, expert installation, and exceptional customer service throughout the entire state.

What sets Merlord apart is our commitment to quality craftsmanship and attention to detail. Every shower door we install is backed by our lifetime warranty, giving you peace of mind with your investment.
